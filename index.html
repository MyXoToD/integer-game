How To Play

1. Wait until at least 3 numbers appeared

2. Click the right numbers to remove them

3. If you hit a wrong button a random number will be added immediately

4. If you are able to clear all the numbers your level will be increased by one

5. The higher your level the faster new numbers will appear

6. If there is a number multiple times you can remove all of them with one button click
